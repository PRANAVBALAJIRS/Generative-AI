Why is it important to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all?\nAnswer: Ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all is important for several reasons:

1. **Economic Development:** Access to energy is crucial for economic growth and development. It enables businesses to operate efficiently, creates job opportunities, and improves productivity.

2. **Quality of Life:** Energy is essential for basic needs such as lighting, heating, cooking, and access to clean water. It also enhances healthcare services, education, and communication.

3. **Environmental Sustainability:** Using sustainable energy sources helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions and mitigates climate change. It also protects natural resources for future generations.

4. **Social Equity:** Providing energy access to all ensures that no one is left behind. It promotes social inclusion and reduces disparities in living standards.

5. **Innovation and Technology:** Investing in modern energy infrastructure encourages innovation and the development of new technologies, leading to a more efficient and sustainable energy sector.
